Applicable to: Taxpayers who have ceased their business or activity during the year 2023.

Conditions: The taxpayer must have stopped their business or activity entirely and clearly demonstrate this cessation.

Montant ou plafond: No specific amount or cap is mentioned for this exemption.

Formule de calcul applicable: The revenues corresponding to the period of cessation are not included in the calculation of the savings account deposit base.

Verification: To ensure eligibility, it must be demonstrated that the business or activity was indeed ceased entirely during 2023 and appropriate documentation should be provided as proof.
