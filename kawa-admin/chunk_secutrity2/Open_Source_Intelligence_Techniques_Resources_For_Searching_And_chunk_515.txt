Google removed its original blog search in 2014. It was quite helpful and focused mostly on
personal websites, especially those with a blogging platform. Today, this is nothing more than a
subsection of Google News. You can load the Blogs option under the “All News” menu within
the Tools option on any Google News results page. Alternatively, you can navigate to the
following address, replacing TEST with your search terms.

google.com/search?Pqg=TEST&tbm=nws&tbs=nrt:b
