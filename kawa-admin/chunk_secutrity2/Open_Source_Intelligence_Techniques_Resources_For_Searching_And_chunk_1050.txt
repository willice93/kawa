Pic Requests (reddit.com/r/picrequests)

A constant frustration in my work is blurry, out of focus, or grainy digital images. Commonly, I
will receive surveillance photos that are too dark or light to identify anything of value in the image.
Occasionally, I will find images on social networks that could be beneficial if they were just a
touch clearer. Pic Requests saves the day. This Sub-Reddit consists of digital photo experts
