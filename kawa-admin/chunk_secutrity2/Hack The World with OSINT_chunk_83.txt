2 How to Setup your OSINT Lab

There are some essential tools required to set up your OSINT lab.
They aren’t too complicated and shouldn’t cost any additional money.
Some of the tools do have commercial versions. If you download
anything, check the hash MD5 + SHA-1 or SHA-2 256 or above. MD5
is too short, so should not be relied on solely. Also, check files with
