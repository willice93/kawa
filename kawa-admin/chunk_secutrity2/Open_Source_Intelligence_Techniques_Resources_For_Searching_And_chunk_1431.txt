The Google view on the left will also contain a search field in the lower left
corner. Searching an address in this field will center both maps on the same address. This will
provide a comparison of the satellite images stored on each service. This can quickly identify the
service that has the better imagery. While this can provide a quick side-by-side comparison,
upcoming automated solutions are preferred.
