erisk Operator (*)

The asterisk (*) represents one or more words to Google and is considered a wild card. Google
treats the * as a placeholder for a word or words within a search string. For example, “osint *
training” tells Google to find pages containing a phrase that starts with “osint” followed by one
or mote wotds, followed by “training”. Phrases that fit this search include: “osint video training”
and “osint live classroom training”.
