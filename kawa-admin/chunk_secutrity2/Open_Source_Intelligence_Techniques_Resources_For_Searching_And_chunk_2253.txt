Retail Businesses

The next time you are shopping at a large store such as Walmart or a clothing store such as Old
Navy, pay attention to the employees. Most of them will either have portable radios in their
pockets or wireless headsets. This is how they communicate to request more cashiers, announce
a lunch break, verify a price, and direct employees to different ateas. When you order food at the
drive-through
