Resurrect Pages (addons.mozilla.org/en-US/ firefox/addon/resutrect-pages /)

This extension provides a link to archived versions of websites whenever a page has been
modified, is unavailable, or has been deleted. Right-clicking on any site will offer a new menu
option of “Resurrect this page”. That option will present the following archive services.

Google Cache: A standard cache of the target address from Google

Google Cache Text: The text-only view of a standard Google cache
