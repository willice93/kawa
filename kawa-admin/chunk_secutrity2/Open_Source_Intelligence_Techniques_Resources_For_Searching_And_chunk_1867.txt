Legacy (legacy.com)

There are many websites that search for death related information such as social security indexes
and ancestry records. A leader in this area is Legacy. This site indexes online obituaries and
memorials from approximately 80 percent of all online newspapers. The search on this site is
straightforward and results can identify family members and locations.
