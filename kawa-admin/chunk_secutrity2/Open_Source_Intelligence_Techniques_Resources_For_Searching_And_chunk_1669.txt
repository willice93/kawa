TV News Archive (archive.org/details/tv)

At the time of this writing, the TV News Archive, another part of archive.org, had collected
1,452,000 television news broadcast videos from 2009-2018. Furthermore, it extracts the closed
captioning text from each video and provides a search option for this data. This allows you to
search for any words
