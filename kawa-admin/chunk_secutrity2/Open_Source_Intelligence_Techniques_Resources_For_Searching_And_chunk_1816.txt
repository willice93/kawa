You may want to know the IP address of the person you are researching as provided by their
internet service provider. This address could be used to verify an approximate location of the
person; to provide law enforcement details that would be needed for a court order; or to
determine if multiple email addresses belong to the same subject. All of those scenarios will be
explained here while I explain the various services that can be used.
