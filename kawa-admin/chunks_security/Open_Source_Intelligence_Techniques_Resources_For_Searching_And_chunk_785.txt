TTER Twitter is a social network and micro blogging service that limits most posts to 140 characters. In 2017, Twitter reported that there were 500 million Twitter posts, or “Tweets”, posted every day. Basically, users create a profile and post Tweets announcing their thoughts on a topic, current location, plans for the evening, or maybe a link to something that they feel is important.
