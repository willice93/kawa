Literally translated as “Everyone’s Website”, Renren is a Chinese remake of Facebook. It is one of the most popular Chinese social networks. Users earn points for activities such as logging in, posting status messages, commenting, and receiving comments. As users earn points, their level on the site increases, which unlocks special emoticons, profile skins, and the ability to go “invisible” and view other users’ profiles without their knowledge.
