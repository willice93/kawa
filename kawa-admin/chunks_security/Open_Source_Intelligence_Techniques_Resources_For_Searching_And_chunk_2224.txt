570 General Mobile Radio Service (GMRS) Additional public frequencies, known as General Mobile Radio Service (GMRS) frequencies require a license to legally transmit audio. Most people ignore this requirement and it is seldom enforced. The radios that transmit on these frequencies can use up to 50 watts, allowing the signal to travel farther.
