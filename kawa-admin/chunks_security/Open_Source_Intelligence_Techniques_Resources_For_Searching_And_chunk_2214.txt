There are two paths you can take in reference to hardware requirements to monitor the airwaves. Dedicated devices, such as police scanners and desktop receivers, are easier to configure. A Software Defined Radio (SDR) is more complicated and requires a computer. However, the monitoring possibilities with an SDR are greater.
