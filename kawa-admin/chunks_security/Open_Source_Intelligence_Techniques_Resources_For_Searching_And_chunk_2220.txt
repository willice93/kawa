Family Radio Service (FRS) If you go to the local department store and purchase a pair of two-way family radios, you will be transmitting on a public radio frequency that can be monitored with a scanner. The Family Radio Service (FRS) is a fadio system authorized for use without a license since 1996. There are 14 FRS channels available which operate on 14 specific frequencies.
