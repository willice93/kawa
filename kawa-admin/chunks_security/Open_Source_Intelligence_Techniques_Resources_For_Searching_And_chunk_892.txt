In addition to Tweets, the Twitter Archiver app will also import other data including the Tweet’s reTweet & favorite count, the user’s friend & follower count, and whether they are verified or not. If you would like to stop archiving Tweets for a particular search term, go to the Twitter Archiver menu, choose the Saved Searches menu, and you’ll see a list of your existing saved searches.
